Who is the Grimace Bunny?

the Grimace Bunny is an animal that is related to the blue smurf cat, the strawberry elephant, and the pineapple owl. It is vegetarian, so it only eats grimace shakes. it is the fourth and final member of the meme animals, which includes smurf cat, the strawberry elephant, and the pineapple owl.

The story of Grimace Bunny

A few years ago, a bunny was born, but he was weird, he was purple and fat.Because of his weird looks, he was abandoned by his family in the middle of the forest

Grimace Bunny grew up

After a few lonely days, Grimace Bunny decided to go on his own path
He started hunting wild animals in the forest, getting better at it every day.
He ate so much protein, and he turned into a monster

He found out there's a new world outside the forest.

After years of living in the forest, hunting everything possible, he became the Alpha.
The forest was soon exhausted of food and protein. He started exploring outside.
*There were lights everywhere*. Grimace Bunny was scared. His wild spirit couldnt handle the shining city, with building as tall as him, the poluted air and loud noises
But wait, what is that sweet smell?? Grimace bunny followed that smell untill he found a restaurant selling a purple raspberry shake called "Grimace shake".

The fight

Soon, Grimace Bunny will find out he is not the only massive creature.
His presence was sensed by a prehistoric monster named Godzilla, the only king of monsters undefeated for thousands of years.
Godzilla started fighting Grimace Bunny, who was terified, but somehow happy, because a monster of his size existed
The fight was long, the entire Tokyo city was destroyed...

The end

They were way too powerful, Godzilla's radioactive blast did no harm to Grimace Bunny, who was fueled by the sugar inside the Grimace Shake.
Godzilla's defence was unbeatable, his massive spikes were hurting Bunny most of the times. The massive giants, exhausted, realized this fight will never end...
Godzilla went back to his underground cave to heal, while Grimace Bunny decided to stay in the Tokyo city to help with rebuilding the place.
